About US

Welcome to our website! We are middle-schoolers from Henrico County, and we are pleased to show you our webpage. Our website mainly focuses on space exploration, and specifically the “Mission to Mars”. We specify certain agencies and corporations working on the Mars program, like NASA and SpaceX. Our website includes an interactive timeline, information about manned and unmanned space missions, two multimedia interviews with employees from NASA and Drapers Laboratory, career fields that NASA focuses on explicitly, and positive/negative impacts of space exploration. We appreciate this wonderful opportunity to enrich web development skills as well as researching skills.
